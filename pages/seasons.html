How I Met Your Mother — 9 Seasons

Season 1(2005–2006)

Ted decides to look for true love after Marshall gets engaged. We meet Robin, Barney, Lily, and the beginning of the adventures at MacLaren’s bar. The episode “Pilot” sets the tone of comedy and friendship that will follow throughout the series.

Season 2 (2006–2007)

Ted and Robin finally get together, while Marshall and Lily deal with marriage. Barney continues his antics, but also shows a more emotional side. Humor and romance are mixed in a balanced way.

Season 3 (2007–2008)

Ted and Robin break up, and both try to move on. Ted begins to rethink what he really wants in a relationship, while Barney has an unexpected involvement with Robin. A more mature tone emerges.

Season 4 (2008–2009)

Marshall and Lily consolidate their life as a couple, Ted starts his own architecture company, and Barney begins to realize true feelings for Robin. Memorable episodes show the group's growth.

Season 5 (2009–2010)

Robin and Barney try a relationship, but discover that they might be better off as friends. Ted starts teaching at the university, getting closer and closer to the moment when he will meet 'the mother.'

Season 6 (2010–2011)

Ted gets involved with Zoey, an activist who opposes the project he is working on. Lily and Marshall try to have a child. Emotional moments and major twists set the stage for the conclusion.

Season 7 (2011–2012)

Robin and Barney grow closer again, while Lily finally becomes pregnant. Ted revisits the past and reflects on his choices. The series continues to mix humor and emotion in an engaging way.

Season 8 (2012–2013)

The stories begin to converge. Marshall and Lily learn to be parents, Barney plans the wedding with Robin, and Ted is about to meet the love of his life. The atmosphere is one of farewell and growth.

Season 9 (2013–2014)

The entire season takes place during Barney and Robin's wedding weekend, culminating in Ted meeting Tracy, the mother. The final episode closes a cycle of love, friendship, and destiny that lasted nine years.
