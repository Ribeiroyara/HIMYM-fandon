Sinopse de How I Met Your Mother

"How I Met Your Mother" follows the life of Ted Mosby and his friends in New York City, as Ted tells his children the story of how he met their mother. Between dates, jobs, romantic adventures, and misadventures, the group of Ted, Robin, Marshall, Lily, and Barney faces the joys and challenges of adulthood.

Over 9 seasons, the series combines comedy, romance, and heartfelt moments, exploring themes of friendship, love, relationships, and destiny. The narrative is non-linear, full of flashbacks and flashforwards, making each episode unique and packed with clever humor and cultural references.

Iconic moments like the "Slap Bet," the pineapple mystery, Robin Sparkles, and Barney's marriage proposal make the series memorable, creating a loyal fanbase that follows the stories of love and friendship until the emotional finale.
